About fable

Fable is the go-to destination for students who want to succeed in their studies. We offer a comprehensive suite of resources that cover everything from choosing the right school and course, to taking notes and acing exams.

Fable was founded in 2022 by two students who saw the need for a one-stop-shop when it came to planning for and succeeding in post-secondary education. We understand the challenges and stresses that come with being a student, and we're here to help you overcome them!
Our website is packed with articles, tips, and tricks that will help you every step of the way, from your first day of classes to your last exam. So whether you're just starting out on your educational journey, or you're looking for some extra help to get over the hump, Fable is here for you!
